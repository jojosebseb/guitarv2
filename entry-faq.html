Sebastian Guitars Fingerstyle Competition Entry Guide
General Rules
Sebastian Guitars are having a Fingerstyle Competition for the guitar lovers out there. The winner of the competition will receive a Sebastian Guitars Voucher worth IDR 8,500,000, a wild card will winner will receive a Sebastian Guitars Voucher worth IDR 2,500,000.
The marking scheme of this competition is broken down in to 3 parts, technicality, originality, and crowd favourite, each worth of 45%, 45%, and 10% respectfully to the overal scroll. Technical and originality marks will be assesed by two judges. One from Sebastian Guitars, and another special guest which will be revealed in the end of the competition, while crowd favourite is based on the number of shares.
  • The competition is open to everyone residing in Indonesia.
  • The submission starts at 25th of December and ends at 25th of January.
  • The winners will be announced 1 week after the closing date in our website and instagram page.
  • The grand prize is worth IDR 8,500,000 and the wild card prize is worth IDR 2,500,000.
  • The contestant which receive the most number of likes and shares will be awarded as the wild card winner.
  • The vouchers are non-refundable.
Step 1 - Get an Entry Voucher
An Entry Code is required for the video to be officially registered. You can get yours here, or head over to our instagram to check if we're having a giveaway.
  • The entry code is not locked to one person, therefore it is transferrable.
  • The entry code is non-refundable.
Step 2 - Upload A Video to Youtube
The video should contain you playing an acoustic / classical guitar playing a fingerstyle arrangement. Video effects may be added, however audio enhancing effects such as reverb, bass boost, or chorus and etc are not allowed.
  • The video must be at least 1 minute long and not exceed 5 minutes.
  • It is adviced to have the least noise or interference possible for the video.
  • Your face, guitar and fingers must be shown inside the video.
  • The video title should be formated as, Sebastian Fingerstyle Competition : "Full Name" - "Song Title"
  • The video must be published during the given time period to be elligible.
  • Please include the composer and arranger of the song, if you are the composer/arranger please state your name.
  • We are entitled to request a re-upload if a problem exists in the video, in which the entry code can be reused again.
Step 3 - Register Your Video
After you succesfully uploaded your video to YouTube, head over to the register page and fill out the form.
  • An entry code can only be used for one submission.
